Habitwo
Application for the creation of habits between two people
PROYECT.

For this project, I had the challenge of designing an experience that would help create habits in couples. Being a product designed from scratch, it was also necessary to create the brand identity and its respective design system. As we were looking for a quick test and launch, we worked in a modality similar to a hackathon, which consisted of an intensive work of approximately 3 days.

ROL.

Within this project I was in charge of designing the brand identity as well as the design system. Once this was done, I dedicated myself to the design of the product experience and interface.

Main tasks.

Definition of color palette and typographies | Design of logo and isotype | Definition of patterns for design system | Design of experience | Design of interface

MOODBOARD

The moodboard was inspired by illustrations and shapes with solid, bright and cheerful colors.
